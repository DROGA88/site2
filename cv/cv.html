Miko Revereza Curriculum Vitae

(b. 1988. Manila, Philippines)
miko.revereza@gmail.com

Miko Revereza is an award winning experimental filmmaker who has made a series of personal documentaries informed by his experiences with migration and exile; DROGA! (2014), Disintegration 93-96 (2017), No Data Plan (2018), Distancing (2019), El Lado Quieto (2021) and Nowhere Near (2023). These works have been screened at festivals and institutions such as Locarno, TIFF, NYFF, and MoMA. No Data Plan is recognized with such honors as the Sheffield Doc Fest Art Award, as well as being listed in BFI Sight & Sound Magazine’s 50 Best Films of 2019, Hyperallergic’s Top 12 Documentary and Experimental Films of 2019 and CNN Philippines Best Filipino Films of 2019. Nowhere Near (recipient of Hubert Bals Fund) was among Film Comment Best Undistributed Films of 2023 and CNN Philippines Best Filipino Films of 2023. Revereza was included in Filmmaker Magazine’s New Faces of Independent Cinema, a Flaherty Seminar featured filmmaker, and holds an MFA from Bard College. In 2025 Miko and his partner Carolina Fusilier founded Cinema Antena, a micro-cinema in San Agustín Etla, Oaxaca.
