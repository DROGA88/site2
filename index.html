Welcome to my log cabin in the woods of the internet, where I'll retreat from the platform apocalypse of our brain rott generation into further exile from the centralized metropolises of digital citizenship and inhabit this space that I built with rudimentary methods as a mode of algorithmic resistence and towards a notion of technoprovinciality. This site houses a living, growing archive of my daily (moving) image practice, functioning as a publishing labratory and reference library to my research.
