DISINTEGRATION 93-96 (DCP, 6min, Color, 2017)

An essay film about the Filipino American undocumented experience, retracing connections between the dream depicted in a home movie and the later disintegration of the traditional family. The film reflects on the act of recording a film document without the possession of living documents.
